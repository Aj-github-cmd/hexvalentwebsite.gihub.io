About Us

Hexavalent established with the idea of selling Textile Auxiliaries. The main focus of the company is to offer selected products for meeting the desired requirements of the industrial customers. We believe and follow ethical business practices to enhance customer experience.

Our Moto is to have strong and friendly customer relationship. We make sure that products, we serve are environment friendly. Hexavalent ensures constant performance, timely delivery and service reliability. We have a dedicated and committed team to inhance our customer experience with utmost sincerity.

Currently, within the period of just three years we have emerged as a well accepted product distribution and formulations name among our customers. Our present product portfolio is limited to selected use in the industry for catering to satisfaction of the consumers. The near future plan to expand the product range with satisfactory network reach and ethical business practices.

R&D

Hexavalent is a Research & Development Company renowned for Manufacturing Speciality Chemical products. With Client-Centric approach, we at Hexavalent have nurtured long-term relationships with our Clients, thus acquiring a huge clientele in both Domestic and Global Markets.
