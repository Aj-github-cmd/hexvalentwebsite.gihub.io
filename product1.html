Scouring Agent

HEXAVALENT-SN 1000 an economical wetting, scouring, soaping agent and detergent. Excellent lime soap dispersant. Excellent washing off agent after dyeing.

Attributes

  • In higher concentration HEXAVALENT -SN-1000 exhibits excellent stain removing properties.

  • HEXAVALENT -SN -1000 is wetting agent and detergent with an emulsifying action, for removing impurities like oil, fat, wax etc.

  • No unpleasant smell.

  • HEXAVALENT -SN-1000 improves the absorbency of textile materials during the pre-treatment.

  • Minimized organic pollutant on dye house.

Applications

  • Wetting agent/detergent, with pronounced emulsifying properties, which promotes the removal of hydrophobic impurities and pigment soil from cotton, regenerated and synthetic fibres and from wool blends during pre-treatment processes.

  • In higher concentration, the product has a stain-removing action.

Stain Remover

The chemicals in this section can help you get rid of those nasty household and laundry stains when properly used.Â­

Acetic Acid. A 10% solution of acetic acid can be purchased generically at pharmacies. (White vinegar is 5% acetic acid and can be used as a substitute for the stronger solution.) It is a clear fluid that can be used to remove stains on silk and wool. It must be diluted with 2 parts water for use on cotton and linen (a pretest is recommended). It should not be used on acetate. If acetic acid causes a color change, sponge the affected area with ammonia.
